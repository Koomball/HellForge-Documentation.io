Normal Crops

  • All crops listed here can be obtained from the farming shop 24/7, Crops have a chance to be collected at different rarities as shown in the Rarity Chances section of each crop.
  • Index:
    • RR = Rarity Rank (rarity system used for items)
    • Fxp = Farming XP

Crop Index

  • Rarity Rank 1
    • RR 1 | Wheat
    • RR 1 | Corn
  • Rarity Rank 2

[RR = 1] Wheat

The starter plant.
obtained from: /shop

  • Requirements to Grow:
    • Level 5+
    • Level 0+ Farmig
  • Crop Information:
    • Grow Time: 10min.
    • Care Requirement: Must be watered once after planting.
    • Yield: 3 - 12 Wheat.
    • Value: $100 Each.
    • Chance of Dying: 0%
    • Rarity Chances/Breakdown:
      • (⭕) Standard: 75%
      • (⭐️) Uncommon: 20%
      • (⭐️⭐️) Rare: 4%
      • (⭐️⭐️⭐️) Legendary: 1%
