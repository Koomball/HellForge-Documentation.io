Primary Commands

<input> = Non Required.
{input} = Required.

/help - Gives the support server invite link and url to this documenation.
/mine - Opens the HellForge Mining Menu.
/inventory - View your inventory.
/craft - Opens the crafting menu.
^ This command requires the Level 10+ Milestone to be completed.

Economy Commands

<input> = Non Required.
{input} = Required.

/balance - View your networth breakdown.
/deposit {amount} - Deposit the inputted amount into your Bank.
/withdraw {amount} - Withdraw the inputted amount from your Bank.
/shop - View the shop and its items.
/buy {item} {amount} - Buy items from the shop.
